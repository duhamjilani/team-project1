WASH & FOLD LAUNDRY

The ideal service if you want to avoid the dreaded laundry chore and save your time and your sanity.

We do your laundry for you so you can enjoy your free time.
Whether it’s a week’s worth of the entire family’s clothes, or all your linens, we pick up, wash, and fold your laundry.

Dry Cleaning with a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee

Our experienced Dry Cleaning ensure 100% Quality and Eco-friendly cleaning. Our “spotters” have decades of experience in identifying and treating stains with their acquired knowledge of organic chemistry so your garments are returned pristine.

Expert Care For Your Garment's Dry Cleaning

At DryClean Lab we carefully grade and inspect each garment prior to cleaning. ﻿We follow the care label (and know what all the symbols mean!) so your clothes receive the optimal cleaning treatment and last for years to come.

Convenient Dry Cleaning Service Near You!

Your Items are picked up from your door, expertly cleaned, crisply pressed, put on hangers, and returned in your protective garment bag, ready to wear upon delivery.
